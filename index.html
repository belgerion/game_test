BattleTech

The Game of Armored Combat

Welcome to the 31st Century. Humanity has been at war for 300 years, using three story tall robots called BattleMechs or Mechs. Piloted by highly skilled MechWarriors, these Mechs have become the most devestating conventional weapon of war. Carrying an arsenal of laser, missle and ballistic weapons, even the smallest Mech has enough firepower to level a city. You are about to take command of one of these Mechs. Are you ready?
